Chocolate Cake Recipe

Ingredients Quantity
All-purpose flour 2 cups
Granulated sugar 2 cups
Cocoa powder 3/4 cup
Baking powder 2 teaspoons
Baking soda 1 teaspoon
Salt 1 teaspoon
Buttermilk 1 cup
Vegetable oil 1/2 cup
Eggs 2
Vanilla extract 2 teaspoons
Boiling water 1 cup

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Grease and flour two 9-inch (23cm) round baking pans.
  3. In a large bowl, combine flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  4. Add buttermilk, vegetable oil, eggs, and vanilla extract. Beat at low speed for 30 seconds.
  5. Gradually add boiling water and beat until the batter is smooth.
  6. Pour the batter evenly into the prepared pans.
  7. Bake for 30 to 35 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cake comes out clean.
  8. Cool the cakes in the pans for 10 minutes.
  9. Remove the cakes from the pans and cool completely on wire racks.
