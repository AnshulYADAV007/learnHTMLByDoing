Club Sandwich Recipe

Ingredients Quantity
Bread 6 slices
Mayonnaise 4 tablespoons
Mustard 2 tablespoons
Turkey breast 6 ounces
Ham 6 ounces
Bacon 6 strips
Lettuce 6 leaves
Tomato 1, sliced
Salt and pepper To taste

Instructions:

  1. Toast the bread slices.
  2. Mix mayonnaise and mustard in a small bowl.
  3. Spread the mixture on one side of each bread slice.
  4. Add turkey breast, ham, bacon, lettuce, and tomato on one slice of bread.
  5. Season with salt and pepper.
  6. Top with another slice of bread.
  7. Cut the sandwich in half and serve.
